About Me

I am currently majoring in VCD, minoring in Business Administration, and pursuing a certification in UX/UI. In the future I plan on working in the design field, but I haven't decided which specific area of design I'd like to pursue. I initially discovered my love for design while designing my high school yearbook. From there, my passion for design has continued to grow!

In addition to design, my other passions include travel, and collecting/growing rare tropical plants. One day I hope to own a house with enough yard to build a greenhouse! It would be the perfect place for artistic inspiration.
